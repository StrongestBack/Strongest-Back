Our Story

Artisan Coffee Roasters was founded in 1998 by Kiwanik Barry, whose passion for coffee was deeply rooted in his family’s love for the craft. Inspired by the joy of sharing a perfect cup of coffee, Kiwanik set out to create a place where the community could gather and savor the best coffee experience possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional coffee while making a positive impact on our community and the environment. We focus on sustainable sourcing, small-batch roasting, and building meaningful relationships with our growers and customers.

What We Stand For

  • Quality: We carefully source the finest beans from ethical farms around the world.
  • Sustainability: Our commitment to eco-friendly practices drives every decision we make.
  • Community: From the growers we partner with to our loyal customers, relationships are at the heart of what we do.
  • Innovation: We blend traditional roasting methods with modern techniques to craft unforgettable coffee.

Meet the Founder

Kiwanik Barry

A lifelong coffee enthusiast, Kiwanik brings decades of experience and a passion for perfection to every cup of coffee we serve. He believes coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a way to connect with people and share stories.

Giving Back

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. A portion of every sale goes to local initiatives that support sustainable farming, education for coffee growers, and programs that uplift our local community. Every cup of coffee you enjoy helps us make a difference.
